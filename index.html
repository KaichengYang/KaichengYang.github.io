Biography

Hi! I'm Kai-Cheng Yang (杨凯程), the pronunciation is KY-cheng YAHNG. You can also call me Kevin. I'm a third year Ph.D candidate in Informatics at Luddy School of Informatics, Computing, and Engineering in Indiana University Bloomington.

I'm interested in computational social science and network science. I mainly work with Filippo Menczer, Yong-Yeol Ahn and Brea L. Perry.

Before joining the Ph.D program at IU, I received my bachelor and master degrees in theoretical physics from Lanzhou University in China.
