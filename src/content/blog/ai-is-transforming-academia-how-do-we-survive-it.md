## TL;DR
- Surviving the AI transformation requires two things: mastering AI agents and mastering what they can't do well.
- Both require exploring the limits of AI agents---use them frequently and push them beyond what you think is possible.
- Avoid spending time on things AI already does well.

## Dos and Don'ts

I was discussing with some colleagues about how we should train our students given the upcoming transformation of academia. But I think this is bigger than just training students---as professors, we also need to think about how we ourselves can survive the transformation.

And here is what I thought.

### Dos

Here are two principles for surviving this:

1. Be very familiar with AI agents and know how to leverage them for various tasks.
2. Figure out the things AI agents cannot do well, and master those.

Both require exploring the limits of AI agents.
So it's important to:

1. Use AI agents for various tasks as frequently as possible.
2. Push the limits by attempting things you didn't think were possible.

### Don'ts

Following the same principles, we should avoid spending precious time on things that AI already does or will soon do really well.
